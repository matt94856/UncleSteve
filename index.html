Oil Changes

Potential Indicators:

  • Engine running rough or noisy
  • Dirty or dark-colored oil
  • Irregular oil change intervals

Service Details:

  • Drain old oil and replace oil filter
  • Refill with fresh, manufacturer-recommended oil
  • Perform a visual inspection of the vehicle

Tire Rotation

Potential Indicators:

  • Uneven tire wear
  • Vehicle pulling to one side
  • Tire pressure irregularities

Service Details:

  • Remove and inspect tires
  • Rotate tires to ensure even wear
  • Inflate tires to recommended pressure

Brake Service

Potential Indicators:

  • Squeaking or grinding noise while braking
  • Reduced braking performance
  • Dashboard brake warning light

Service Details:

  • Inspect brake pads, calipers, and rotors
  • Replace brake pads and related parts as needed
  • Perform brake fluid flush if necessary

Minor Air Conditioning Repair

Potential Indicators:

  • No cool air or weak airflow from vents
  • Strange odor coming from the air conditioning system
  • Unusual noises when the system is running

Service Details:

  • Perform a thorough inspection of the A/C system
  • Recharge refrigerant if necessary
  • Repair leaks and replace faulty components

Tune-up Services

Potential Indicators:

  • Difficulty starting the engine
  • Engine misfires or runs rough
  • Decreased fuel efficiency

Service Details:

  • Diagnose ignition system components (spark plugs, coils, etc.)
  • Replace faulty spark plugs and ignition coils

Fuel Injection System Service

Potential Indicators:

  • Decreased fuel efficiency
  • Engine hesitation or stalling
  • Surging or rough idling

Service Details:

  • Perform a thorough cleaning of fuel injectors
  • Clean the throttle body and intake valves
  • Remove deposits to improve fuel efficiency and performance
